Facilities

Discover an Oasis of Unparalleled Luxury & Comfort at Wayanad Silverwoods

Start Your Comfortable Stay

Explore a range of premium facilities crafted for your comfort and delight.

Surrounded by towering mountains and calm, reflective waters, our resort provides everything you need for a peaceful and fulfilling stay.

KARLAD - Infinity Pool

…Repose & unwind…
Lounge with a book at Wayanad Silverwoods pool area overlooking the Banasura Peak. An exotic view from the pool side to Banasura Sagar and hills will enthrall your mind and eyes. We conduct pool side dinner and conferences under the backdrop of serene nature of Wayanad. Take the plunge into the Pool that beckons you with its clear azure waters in the scenic location.
We also have a Kiddie’s pool area which is the dedicated space designed for our youngest guests to splash, play and enjoy endless hours of water-fun.

Banasura – Dining

…Taste the Passion, Savor the Destination…
Our Multi-cuisine Restaurant serve lip smacking dishes of your choice, be it Continental, Mughlai, North Indian or South Indian. Specially trained hospitality team will be ready to serve you the special beverages, feasts and delicacies. Our Restaurant provides you the most stunning view of the woods while relishing on the delicious treats.
Our team of chefs takes pride in using the freshest local ingredients, blending traditional recipes with modern techniques to create a menu that delights and surprises. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely breakfast buffet, a sophisticated lunch, or an exquisite dinner, our restaurant offers an inviting setting where every meal becomes an occasion.

Ayur Sparsh – Ayurvedic Spa

…Revitalize & rejuvenate…
Indulge in the ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation experience at our luxurious Ayur Spa. Embedded within the serene surroundings of our resort, the spa offers a sanctuary where you can unwind, destress and revitalize your mind, body and spirit. Our professional therapists and state- of- the- art facilities ensure an exceptional spa experience tailored to your individual needs. Embrace the essence of holistic wellness as you indulge in our luxurious spa experiences, set against the backdrop of nature’s beauty.

Lakkidi – The Club House

…Where Memories are Made, and Laughter Echoes…
Welcome to the heart of our resort, Lakkidi -The Clubhouse – your ultimate retreat for relaxation and camaraderie. Step into a world where laughter fills the air, and every moment is an opportunity for connection and celebration. Our Clubhouse offers a perfect blend of elegance and comfort, featuring plush seating areas, an inviting bar stocked with top-shelf spirits, and a variety of recreational activities to suit all tastes. Whether you're here to unwind with a drink, engage in friendly competition with a game of billiards, or simply enjoy the company of friends and family, our Clubhouse provides a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes every gathering memorable. Experience the joy of leisure and the delight of shared moments at the centerpiece of your resort stay.

Outdoor Experience

...A day on trails...
For those seeking a more leisurely experience, guided nature walks provide a closer look at the local flora and fauna, cycling trails that wind through scenic landscape,while our pottery-making experience invite guests to explore their creativity in a tranquil outdoor setting.

Kids Play Area

…Where Fun and Adventure Await Little Explorers…
Our vibrant and secure Kids' Play Area is designed to be a haven of fun and imagination, where children can embark on countless adventures and explore their creativity. Spanning a spacious and well-equipped environment, the play area features a variety of activities that cater to different age groups and interests. From brightly colored slides and climbing structures to interactive games and play zones, there is something to captivate every child's attention and keep them entertained for hours.

Events & Meetings

…Elevating your events and Meetings …
Our conference hall facility is meticulously designed to meet all your business and event needs with style and efficiency. our space is ideal for hosting conferences, seminars, workshops, and corporate meetings. Our experienced support team is always on hand to assist with any technical or logistical requirements, ensuring your event runs seamlessly from start to finish. With convenient location and ample parking, our conference hall is the perfect choice for making your event a resounding success.

Destination Wedding

…A dream wedding in an idyllic setting…
At our resort, we understand that a wedding is more than just a ceremony; it is a celebration of love and the beginning of a lifetime of memories. Our dedicated team is committed to curating an experience that reflects your unique love story, ensuring that every detail, from the decor to the dining, is meticulously crafted to perfection. Whether you envision a grand celebration or an intimate gathering, our idyllic setting provides the perfect canvas for your wedding dreams. With luxurious accommodations, exquisite cuisine, and personalized service, we go above and beyond to ensure that your special day is nothing short of extraordinary. Let us be a part of your love story, and together, we will create a wedding experience that you and your guests will cherish forever. At our resort, your dream wedding in an idyllic setting awaits.

Pet- Friendly

…Where Pets Play, Stay, and Feel Loved…
Our pet-friendly accommodations ensure that your pets can relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings just as much as you do. With ample outdoor spaces perfect for long walks and playful exploration, your pets will have plenty of opportunities to stretch their legs and enjoy the fresh air. We have designated pet-friendly areas where they can safely roam and play, and you’ll find scenic trails and open spaces ideal for shared adventures. Whether you’re exploring the resort’s lush grounds or simply unwinding in your room, your pets are welcome to be part of your memorable getaway.

Fitness Centre

…Stay Fit in Paradise…
Our Fitness Centre at the resort is well equipped with Treadmills, Bicycles, Weight Stations, Free Weights, Elliptical Cross Trainers, Exercise balls and Mats. These equipment can energize and refresh you throughout the stay at Wayanad Silverwoods.
Silverwoods is the top-rated Resorts with Fitness Centre in Wayanad and a scrupulously clean and extremely comfortable gym for fast, convenient, and affordable workout. The Fitness Centre is open everyday, and is a great place to come and relax after a long day exploring the area. The Fitness Centre is well-equipped with a variety of cardio machines, free weights, and body weight equipment.
